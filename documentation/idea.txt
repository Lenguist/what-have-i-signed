# What Have I Signed?

## Core Idea
A browser extension (Chrome/Firefox) that automatically captures Terms & Conditions, Privacy Policies, and other legal agreements whenever you sign up for a service or click "I Accept." All captured documents are saved to a database so you can revisit them later.

## Key Features (in priority order)

### Phase 1 - Capture & Store
- Detect T&C/ToS/Privacy Policy text on sign-up pages automatically
- Save the document with metadata: service name, date accepted, URL
- User account/dashboard to browse all accepted agreements

### Phase 2 - AI Chat & Summarization
- Chat with any saved agreement using AI ("What data do they collect about me?")
- On-demand plain-English summary of any document
- Highlight the most important/unusual clauses

### Phase 3 - Proactive Flagging
- Flag suspicious or aggressive clauses BEFORE you click accept
- Warn about: data selling, arbitration clauses, auto-renewal traps, broad IP ownership grabs, etc.

### Phase 4 - Actions
- Draft withdrawal-of-consent emails to companies
- Track which companies have updated their T&Cs since you accepted
- Notify you when terms change

## Why It Matters
Nobody reads the 10,000-word wall of legal text before clicking "I Accept." This service makes AI read it for you — both proactively (before you sign) and retrospectively (after the fact).

## MVP Scope
1. Chrome extension that detects and captures T&C text on sign-up flows
2. Backend that stores documents per user
3. Simple chat interface (powered by Claude API) to query any saved document
